CapSoul

When was the last time that you turned your head in the street after a trail of a familiar smell?

Compared to the other senses, our smell sense has a bigger ability to arouse memories and emotions fast and intense. The human body scent is responsible for the formation of the first connection between a fresh parent and a newborn, on the attraction between couples and our last tangible to deceased.

CapSoul is combined out of four objects that holds the power to preserve the smell of our loved ones and allows as to remember them and fill then when we miss them.

For a parent and newborn there is the pajamas shirt and baby pajamas overall that can be fold into a special pocket that transform them to poppets. The baby can be left during the day with the parent shirt in the shape of a puppet and with his smell and the parent can carry the baby's smell in a smell puppet too.
For the lovers there is also a designed pajamas piece one for each that can be placed in its 3d printed case that with a light pressure can open up for the user to smell.
For the relative of the deceased there is the aluminum container for his/her pillowcase with 7 openings and a lead to close them all.
